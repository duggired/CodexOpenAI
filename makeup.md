To identify the specific hotels and resorts contacts in the Tampa area, use the following methods:
1. Utilize Official Hotel Websites 
The most direct way to find contacts is through the official websites of the hotels and resorts. 
Target the Right Page: Look for "Contact Us," "Meetings & Events," "Weddings," or "Plan Your Event" sections.
Identify the Manager: These pages often list specific email addresses or phone numbers for the sales and events team (e.g., "eventsales.tpa@editionhotels.com").
Look for Names: Scan the page for titles like "Event Manager," "Catering Manager," "Wedding Coordinator," or "Director of Events." This gives you a specific person to address in your email.
General Phone Number: If a direct email isn't available, call the main hotel number and ask the receptionist for the name and email address of the Wedding or Event Coordinator. 
2. Use Professional Networking Sites (LinkedIn)
LinkedIn is an excellent tool for finding specific people within an organization.
Search by Company: Go to LinkedIn and search for the specific hotel or resort.
Filter by People: Once on their company page, click on the "People" tab.
Use Job Titles as Keywords: Search within their employees for titles such as "Event Coordinator," "Event Manager," "Catering Sales Manager," "Director of Events," or "Wedding Specialist" in the Tampa area.
Connect Professionally: Once you find the right person, you can connect with them and send a brief, professional message. 
3. Leverage Wedding and Event Planning Directories
Websites catering to wedding planning often list venues and their affiliated event planners. 
Targeted Directories: Use sites like WeddingWire, The Knot, Eventective, or PartySlate.
Find "Preferred Vendor" Lists: Many venues on these sites provide lists of "preferred vendors" (like you want to become). You can connect with those existing vendors to network and sometimes find event manager details.
Venue Profiles: Check the venue's profile page on these sites; they sometimes list a direct contact or link to their event planning page. 
4. Consult Local Convention & Visitors Bureaus (CVB)
Organizations like Visit Tampa Bay offer resources for event planners and sometimes list contacts or information on local venues.
Visit Tampa Bay Website: Their "Meeting Planners" or "Suppliers" sections might have a list of venues and potentially some contact information.
Networking Events: These organizations often host networking events where you can meet hotel and resort staff in person. 
5. Utilize Search Engines
A targeted Google search can sometimes yield quick results.
Specific Queries: Use searches like:
"JW Marriott Tampa Water Street" "event manager email"
"The Don CeSar" "wedding coordinator contact"
"Hotel Flor Tampa" "catering manager"
By using a combination of these methods, you can build a targeted list of contacts for a more effective and personalized outreach.
